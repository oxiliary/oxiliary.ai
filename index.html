oxiliary is in phase 1 as of November 2020

oxiliary is building an audio intelligence platform to promote music discovery

think WolframAlpha but for music.


proof of concept roadmap

phase 1: enable submission of music and viewing of similar songs based on chosen properties such as emotion, instrument selection, and lyrical content

phase 2: release threads, the perfect audiovisual experience for music discovery

phase 3: provide artists with insights to the tastes of their fanbase
